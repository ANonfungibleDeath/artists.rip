This is a place of mourning

These are the names of those who have died a nonfungible death.

We are gathered here to mourn the loss of those people whoe work once inspired us, but who have been lost to the great monster of NFTs. Artist who we loved, artists who made something great once, artists we feel we've lost. May they rest now and may we who survive them continue to create, to love, and to take care of our small world.
